For some fixed $0<q<1/2$ choose two more points $c, d$
within $[a,b]$ by
$$
c = a + q\cdot(b-a)
$$
$$
d= b - q\cdot(b-a)
$$
That is, $c$ is chosen by going $q(b-a)$ to the right of $a$ and
$d$ is chosen by going $q(b-a)$ to the left of $b$.
